Introduction

This variation on Blood on the Clocktower has the gimmick of deciding roles by asking its players 3 questions, answered from 1 to 5, before starting the match. Depending on how your answers are similar / different to your fellow players decides whether you become a Townsfolk, Outsider or Minion/Demon. The storyteller should aim to pick the Minions & Demon as a group of players with opinions dissimilar to majority but, ideally, similar to each other. Outsiders should be people with an opinion dissimilar to the group, but not necessarily similar to each other. Townsfolk is everyone who wasn't classified as strange in some way!

Townsfolk

Washerwoman (Washerwoman Token)

You start knowing 1 of 2 people has a particular good (similar to rest) opinion.

Librarian (Librarian Token)

You start knowing 1 of 2 people have opinions different to the masses.

Investigator (Investigator Token)

You start knowing 1 of 2 people has a particular evil opinion.

Chef (Chef Token)

You find out how many pairs of outsiders/minions/imps there are

The Host (Empath Token)

At night, hold up a finger from 1 to 3, you find out the opinions of the people next to you on that question.

Fortune Teller (Fortune Teller Token)

Acts normally.

Undercrust (Underwhatever Token)

When someone is executed, you find out all of their opinions.

Bill Splitter (Monk Token)

Each night*, choose a player (not yourself): If they are attacked by the demon, they learn one opinion of the attacker.

Pigeon (Ravenkeeper Token)

If you die at night, choose one person, you learn all of their opinions.

The Picky Eater (Virgin Token)

Choose a question to align to on night 1. If you are nominated by someone with +- 1 as their opinion on that question, they die.

Hater (Slayer Token)

Once per game, you can point at someone and if you know their opinion on one question, they are instantly executed.

Leftover Thief (Soldier Token)

You are safe from the Demon. If you are attacked at night, you learn one opinion of the attacker.

The Franco Manco (Mayor Token)

Once per day, you can gossip and guess everyone's true opinion on one question of your choice. If every opinion is correct, your team wins.

Outsiders

Deliveroo (Butler Token)

One of the townsfolk registers as having the same unusual opinions as you, you know who they are.

Drunk (Drunk Token)

In addition to the usual effect of your power not working, your opinions can also register as whatever the storyteller chooses.

Recluse (Recluse Token)

You might register as having evil opinions, even if dead.

The (Pizza) Pilgrim (Saint Token)

If you die by execution, your team loses.

Minions

Poisoner (Poisoner Token)

Acts normally.

Dominos (Spy Token)

See what everyone submitted to the opinion survey. Your opinions may register as good.

Pizzanovus (Cerenovus Token)

At night, choose one person to make mad that they have an opinion Thumbs Up (= Love, or 5 on survey), Thumbs Down (=Hate, or 1 on Survey) on one of the questions (hold up a finger from 1 to 3).
